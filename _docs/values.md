## Values
Our Values are our core guiding principles. We have two main sets of values: "What We Do" and "Who We Are" values.

### “What We Do” Values:
- Worship
- Belong
- Grow
- Bless

### “Who We Are” Values:
- Love
- Integrity
- Faith
- Truth
